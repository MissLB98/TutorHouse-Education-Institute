PRIVATE TUTORING

The foundation for a solid education is built at school and in today's age it is vital to have a solid platform to launch a career.

We therefore ensure to recruit only the best tutors to assist our students to build that foundation.

  • We provide private tutors for Primary and High School.
  • We cover all CAPS related subjects.
  • And offer additional assistance with a particular subject,concept or even just to assist with homework in general.

GROUP TUTORING

Our learning centre offers Mathematics, Accounting and Physical Science classses for groups of 2 to 10 learners.

This allows for that focused attention that is desperately needed in today's educational environment.

STUDY BUDDY AFTER CARE

  • We offer a faculty that students attend after school, equipped with the necessary resources in order to complete homework in a controlled environment.
  • We have expert tutors available at all times to assist with any academic related questions and assistance with homework.
  • We want to give parents peace of mind knowing that homework will be completed by the time they get home from work.
